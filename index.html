What is a Flood?

A flood is an overflow of water onto land that is normally dry. Floods can occur due to heavy rainfall, melting snow, or dam failure.

Causes of Flooding

  • Heavy Rainfall
  • Rapid Snowmelt
  • Storm Surges
  • Dam or Levee Failure
  • Urban Development

About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to raising awareness about flood prevention and safety. Our mission is to educate communities on how to prepare for and respond to flooding events.
